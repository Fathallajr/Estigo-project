IGCSE Qualification Roadmap in Egypt

Step 1: Choose Your Subjects

Select 5-7 subjects including core subjects (Math, English, Science) and electives

Step 2: Register with British Council

Register for IGCSE exams through the British Council Egypt

Step 3: Study Plan

Create a 2-year study plan covering all syllabus topics

Step 4: Mock Exams

Take practice exams to assess your readiness

Step 5: Final Preparation

Focus on past papers and exam techniques

Step 6: Take Exams

Sit for your IGCSE exams in May/June or October/November sessions
